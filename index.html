About Me

Hi, I'm Shivakumar V, a PhD scholar in Product Design and Engineering at the Department of Design and Manufacturing (DnM, formerly CPDM) at IISc Bangalore. My personal experience as a prosthetic hand user fuels my commitment to developing smarter, more accessible prosthetics. In this space, I share my journey and insights into the cutting-edge innovations at the intersection of Smart Prosthetics, VLSI, and Intent Recognition. My mission is to bridge the gap between human intent and technology, crafting solutions that enhance everyday life while being affordable for underprivileged communities.
