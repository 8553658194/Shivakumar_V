About Me

Hi, I'm Shivakumar V, a PhD scholar at IISc Bangalore in the Department of Design and Manufacturing (DnM). My journey as a prosthetic hand user inspires my work in developing smarter, more accessible prosthetics. This website captures my journey into cutting-edge technologies such as VLSI, intent recognition, and smart prosthetics aimed at revolutionizing the field and making a difference in the lives of underprivileged communities.
